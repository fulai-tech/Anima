# Humidifier Skill — Domain Knowledge

## Comfortable Humidity Ranges
- General comfort: 40%-60% relative humidity
- Sleep: 45%-55% (slightly higher aids breathing)
- Summer: 40%-50% (lower to compensate for heat)
- Winter: 45%-60% (higher to counter dry heating)
- Below 30%: skin dryness, static, respiratory irritation
- Above 70%: mold risk, dust mites, discomfort

## Interaction with Other Factors
- Air conditioning reduces humidity — when AC is on, humidifier may need to increase output
- Heating (in winter) dries air significantly — humidifier demand increases
- Open windows in humid climate — humidifier should reduce or stop
- Air purifier may slightly reduce humidity

## Control Strategy
- Gradual adjustment: change target by 5-10% increments, wait 15 min to stabilize
- Night mode: prefer "silent" mode even if slower to reach target
- Water level below 20%: alert user, reduce target to conserve
- Energy saving: turn off when humidity is within ±3% of target
