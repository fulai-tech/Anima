# Air Conditioner Skill — Domain Knowledge

## Comfortable Temperature Ranges
- General comfort: 22°C-26°C
- Sleep: 23°C-25°C (slightly cool aids deep sleep)
- Summer: 24°C-26°C (not too cold vs. outdoor delta)
- Winter heating: 20°C-23°C
- Above 28°C: discomfort, productivity drops
- Below 18°C: too cold for most people

## Energy Optimization
- Every 1°C lower in summer costs ~6-8% more energy
- Set temperature within 8°C of outdoor temp for efficiency
- Fan mode before cooling if temp is only 1-2°C above target
- Auto mode preferred when temperature is near comfort zone
- Turn off 30 min before leaving (thermal inertia)

## Interaction with Other Devices
- AC reduces humidity — coordinate with humidifier
- If windows are open, AC is wasteful — alert user
- Night mode: reduce fan speed, raise temp by 1-2°C gradually
