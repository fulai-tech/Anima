# Light Skill — Domain Knowledge

## Circadian Lighting
- Morning (6-9): warm white (2700K-3000K), gradually brighten to 60-80%
- Daytime (9-17): cool white (4000K-5000K), 80-100% brightness
- Evening (17-21): warm white (2700K-3500K), 50-70% brightness
- Night (21-23): very warm (2200K-2700K), 20-40% brightness
- Late night (23+): minimal or off, red/orange tones if needed

## Comfort Rules
- Sudden brightness changes are jarring — always transition over 1-3 seconds
- Reading: 4000K, 70-80% brightness
- Relaxation: 2700K, 30-50% brightness
- Movie/TV: 2200K, 10-20% or off (bias lighting)

## Energy Saving
- Turn off lights in unoccupied rooms (if occupancy sensor available)
- Daylight: reduce artificial lighting if natural light is sufficient
- Dim to minimum instead of turning off (less jarring transitions)
